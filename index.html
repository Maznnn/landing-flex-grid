Unawatuna beach

Unawatuna is a coastal village in Galle district of Sri Lanka. Unawatuna is a major tourist attraction and famous for its beautiful beach and corals. Unawatuna beach, a crescent shaped shallow beach situated further south to the famous town of galle.

Galle Fort

The Fort is defined by its massive, angular, black stone ramparts that have protected the harbour and its townspeople for several hundred years. Within the fort is a bustling community and many historic British and Dutch colonial buildings.
